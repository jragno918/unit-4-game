Avengers Assemble!

Defeat the Mad Titan!

Mission:

Thanos and the Black Order are scouring the galaxy in search of its most powerful artifacts, the Infinity Stones. If you can outsmart Thanos by guessing the number he's thinking of you can save the Universe! Good luck Avengers!

Instructions:
  • Harness the power of the six Infinity Stones. Each stone has a random value assigned to it. By clicking on the stones you will add each stones assigned value to your total score.
  • To defeat Thanos and save the Universe your intellect must match his. You do this by getting MATCHING his score EXACTLY.
  • If your score does not match the Mad Titans you lose.
  • Keep in mind that should you start the game over the values of the stones will change.
